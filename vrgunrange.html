VR Gunrange

Assignment

Create a Virtual Reality game of your choice, using Unity3d.

Goals

Complete a functional VR Game within Unity3d.
Challenge your creativity.
Pick a game and develop it using any asset you can find.
Challenge yourself to do something you have not done before.

My process

When I first was assigned the project, I wanted to make a realistic game. After some thinking I decided I wanted to do a gunrange; but not just any gunrange... I wanted to do make a gunrange where the weapon interaction was as realistic as possible, and use real world bullet physics as well. After I settled on what game I wanted I immediately began working on the gun interactions. I started with an M4A1 and was able to get the gun to pickup and shoot fairly easily. Then I started working on the magazine functionality and pulling the slide to cock the gun. I started with a linear method just to get it to work. Once I completed that, I started thinking more latterally and settled on the idea of 'my code should work exactly like a real gun.'
What I mean by this is:

  • The magazine only knows about its current ammo count, how to put bullets into the mag and how to extract a bullet.
  • The gun is seperated into a few components, the slide and the gun itself.
    • The gun script holds the functionallity for weapon handling and how to pull the trigger.
    • The slide knows it's owner (aka the gun) and it knows how to pull a bullet from the inserted mag.
  • The bullets only know how to be shot.

So the player can pick up the gun, then when they pull the slide back, it will try to get a bullet from the magazine and pass it to the gun script. Then the gun script has a chambered round (a bullet ready to fire). When the player pulls the trigger, it tells the chambered round to launch its projectile. And then the bullet fires from the barrel at the correct velocity and when the bullet hits a collider, it will spawn a bullet hole object, then depending on if it was wood or metal that it impacted, it makes the correct impact sound.
