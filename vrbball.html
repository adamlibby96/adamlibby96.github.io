VR Basketball

Assignment

Create a Virtual Reality basketball game inside the Chesapeake Energy Arena for the OKC Thunder.

Goals

Complete a functional VR Game with the Unreal Engine.
Challenge your creativity.
Make a realistic basketball game.
Push yourself to learn different aspects of UE4.

My process

Since this was a team project, we needed to discuss roles and tasks before we began the project. We decided on the art assets we will need, and then we decided how the game will be played and what features we should add to make our game unique. I was the only programmer on the team so I immediately began figuring out how to use UE4 blueprints with the Oculus Rift.
I used the VR template to start out with and quickly figured out that it wasn't what I needed. So this resulted in me having to program it with little refrences. Once I figured out the grabbing, walking, and throwing, I started to work on the gameplay itself. I wanted to game to be fun and playable. So I made it a little easier to throw the basketball into the hoop by adding a collider above the hoop and if the ball hits the collider, it 'snaps' into the hoop.
After I finished the scoring, I made the basketball spawner, and then I added a nice little feature of a live ball and dead ball. A live ball means it can score and trigger other events. The dead ball means that it no-longer effects the game and the ball will be destroyed after a few seconds.
