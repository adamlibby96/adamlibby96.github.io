Pulling out a fresh block of wood with absolutely 0 idea what to carve is a great way to end the day. Figuring out what I am about to carve is the hardest part of the process. But on the otherhand, the more complex the object is, the harder it is to carve. Which makes it more fun and rewarding. I have been carving since this past summer and I feel like I am getting better with each project. It is one of the best stress relievers that I know of. It takes intense focus because you are dealing with sharp objects. I've already had stitches on my finger once, which has caused me to be more cautious on projects.
