About Me

    My name is Adam Libby, I am currently a senior at Oklahoma Christian University in Edmond, OK. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Gaming and Animation. I will be graduating /*hopefully*/ in December, 2019.

    I discovered my love for programming during my senior year in highschool while attending a technical college for networking. My professor gave us two weeks to complete a small program in Java. This lead me to creating a replica of Snake. Obviously being my first time programming, it was the buggiest code I have ever seen. But nonetheless, I loved starting with an empty file and writing some lines of code and watching the project come alive.

    Studying here at OC I have discovered that I really enjoy programming in Unity3d with C#. I started out with making a knock-off of an old TV series American Gladiators. And recently I have found the love for developing VR games. While I enjoy spending time programming and playing video games, I love spending as much time as I can outdoors.

    I enjoy going camping, riding motorcycles, and taking my /*derpy*/ husky, Luna, on runs. In Boy Scouts we always went camping. Every month we had a camping trip or some other activity.

    I was elected Crew Chief for a high-adventure camp called Philmont. As Crew Chief I led a twelve-man crew on a 111-mile backpacking trek. That trip was easily one of my greatest accomplishments. After Philmont, I put together my Eagle project at our local ASPCA. Our job was to clean out their backyard/junkyard, mow and weed eat, and build a few shelves for storage. My team and I put well over 150 man-hours completing this project. I achieved my Eagle Scout rank in 2014.
